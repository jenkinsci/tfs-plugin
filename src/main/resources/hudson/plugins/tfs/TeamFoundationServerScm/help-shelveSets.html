A list of TFS shelvesets to be pulled into the local workspace. Shelvesets are applied to the workspace after getting files.

Every entry must carry the name of the shelveset and the name of its owner separated by a colon (':'), i.e. it must be of the form <name>:<owner>. The owner might be of the simple form or be denoted by domain\name. For examples FeatureNotComplete:Harry or just_for_testing:eudomain\mary. Currently there is no support for spaces in the name or owner. Multiple entries must be delimited by semicolon characters (';'). Entries can be placed onto separate lines, but still require the delimiter.

During unshelving multiple shelvesets, conflicts might be reported in the output. For instance, if two shelvesets address the same file in the workspace. Technically a merge is tried but conflicts are not automatically resolved.
